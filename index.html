Why do you need to check your shopping list?

The more you know :-/


Each year in the United States alone, food-borne disease causes an estimated 48 million illnesses, 128,000 hospitalizations, 3,000 deaths and over 1,000 outbreaks. Food-borne illness has a large economic impact across the nation, representing millions of dollars in lost income, lost revenue and healthcare-associated costs! FDA releases recalled-food data that we can match against your list and tell if any item has been recalled or not.
